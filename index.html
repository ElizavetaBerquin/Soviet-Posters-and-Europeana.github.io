Welcome to Soviet Posters & Europeana!

Press the 'Load the posters!' button once to display the first 500 posters available on the Europeana APIs. You will see all the posters as a single grid of images that is sorted by colors extracted from the metadata. This methos helps to visually explore large numbers of graphic objects and to find patterns in them, while still being able to see each image separately. The main purpose of visualizing the posters this way is to represent many objects as one whole. When all the images are displayed simultaneously it makes it easier to compare them and observe patterns, differences and similarities in composition or color.

Please note it may take a few mintues to load them.
